2022-07-19

Start to make a lot of friends and school gets busy. Learning things. Hope time stops so I have the time to learn everything then I'll have extra time for friends.

I'd say I'm a fairly positive person tho even if I post many maybe confusing and negative things here. I'm both super sensible and emotional, and I have a looota random pessimistic feelings to vent. Most times of the day and on most days in life I feel life is beautiful. At times I feel sadness, for any reason, just sad about life in general, like why's life meaningless and I'm making myself endow it a meaning? Something stupid to think about and funny too.
I'm too sensible to let emotion takes control tho. My sensibleness is just always positive, and whatever I feel, it finds a way to deal with it. It's like "okay now you are sad, do nothing and feel it, your body will produce some serotonin and you'll feel better." I guess that's why sometimes I find it hard to truly empathize with others. It's simply contradictory that I'm very sensitive and emotional, but just never driven by emotions and thus don't know what it is like. It's okay though, a person is just as complicated as that and doesn't make any sense...as something that makes sense :)
Yea that's why I feel I'm genuine and kinda cold. Genuine in an emotional sense, cold is the sensible side. I feel I am myself and nothing and no one else is bound to me. I don't want to have expectations for others, I can't help having them though. I feel everyone else is not important when I am not in the mood, though I feel some are important to me... I feel pressured by unexpected expectations for me, tho I'm also hoping some from some people sometimes. I assume the same for other people.
Everyone cares for themselves and if you have the extra energy to be my friend and care for me, I would do the same and won't withhold any of my enthusiasm or passion or hope for deep connections and everything good from a friendship.
